Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is EduHack Inspire?

EduHack Inspire is a hackathon event focused on the development and innovation in the education system. It brings together creative minds to work on projects that could transform education using technology.

2. Who can participate in EduHack Inspire?

Anyone who is passionate about education, technology, and innovation is welcome to participate. Students, educators, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs can form teams to join the hackathon.

3. How do I register for the event?

4. What is the duration of EduHack Inspire?

The event spans over 24 hours, starting from the kickoff session on the first day and ending with the project presentations and judging on the final day.

5. Is there a prize for the winners?

Yes, there are exciting prizes for the top teams. Winners will receive cash rewards, mentorship opportunities, and exclusive access to educational resources and platforms.

6. Can we participate remotely?

Yes, EduHack Inspire supports both in-person and remote participation. You can join from anywhere, as long as you have a stable internet connection and the required tools for collaboration.

7. How are the projects evaluated?

Projects are evaluated based on innovation, impact, technical implementation, and presentation. Our panel of judges includes industry experts, educators, and technology leaders who will assess each project thoroughly.
